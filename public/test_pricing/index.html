Strategic Position

✦
SpareDollar's Moat
  • Only tool with snap-a-photo → full listing in seconds
  • AI fills title, description, price, category, condition from images
  • No manual copy-paste required — zero friction for first listing
  • 5% starter tier aligns incentives — we only win when you win
  • Free to start with no upfront commitment captures casual sellers
⚡
Why This Pricing Works
  • $89 Pro is $15 cheaper than SellRaze's top tier ($103.99)
  • $29 Growth undercuts every competitor's mid tier
  • 5% entry is unique — no competitor does commission at entry
  • Breakeven at $580/mo in sales → Growth pays for itself
  • "We only take a cut when you sell" is a powerful hook
⚠
Watch Points
  • SellRaze at $104 gives room — don't race to the bottom
  • $89 Pro still needs strong feature differentiation vs $45 OneShop
  • Monitor conversion from Starter → Growth as primary metric
