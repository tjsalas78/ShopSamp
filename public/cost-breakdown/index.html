2 · Claude API — Biggest Variable Cost

Model: claude-sonnet-4-6  ·  Input: $3.00/MTok  ·  Output: $15.00/MTok
Per listing call (optimized): 3 images × 1024px × ~450 tokens = 1,350 + 500 prompt = 1,850 input  ·  ~400 output tokens
Cost: (1,850 × $3 + 400 × $15) / 1,000,000 = $0.00555 + $0.006 = ≈ $0.009 per SpareCredit
Post-optimization (April 2026): Cost dropped ~58% from the pre-optimization baseline ($0.02). Changes: (1) cap at top-3 images per scan, (2) resize client-side to 1024px @ 0.75 JPEG quality, (3) max_tokens reduced from 1500 → 800. Repricing calls (no images) still cost ~$0.004. Tables below are pre-optimization baseline — the live simulator at the bottom uses the new $0.009 rate.

12 · Key Findings & Recommendations

⚠ Business Tier Credit Count
6,000 credits at $149 = $0.025 effective rate — 1.24× cost. At 100% utilization, $120 API vs $149 revenue = 19% gross. Fix: cut Business to 4,000 credits. Effective rate jumps to $0.037, margin at 100% util → 46%.
⚡ Pro is the Money Tier
Pro generates the highest absolute gross dollar margin ($49–$65/user/mo) with a solid margin floor. Prioritize Growth → Pro upgrade. This is where the business makes real money per user.
✓ Overhead is Negligible
$66/mo fixed costs are covered by 2–3 paying users. The business can run experiments and grow at very low paid counts. Risk is API cost compression, not infra.
✓ Overage is High-Margin
Overage credits bill at 3–7× cost. Users who consistently hit limits are your most valuable segment. Consider a "credit pack" add-on for power users who don't want to upgrade tiers.
📊 Watch Free:Paid Ratio
Free tier costs $0.18/user/mo at 60% util. At 1% conversion this is comfortably covered. Set an alert: if free:paid exceeds 30:1, tighten free credits or add email verification to reduce abuse.
📈 Push Annual Billing
Annual subscribers pay upfront and churn at ~half the rate. Growth annual = $390 cash upfront vs $39/mo. The 16.7% discount pays for itself in retained revenue by month 7.
